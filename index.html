Projects

I'm presenting some of my currently active works. Technically, they are not quite relevant. But, these are special for me.

OpenPOTD


It is a bot that is used as the judge of a bi-weekly math contest on discord. I am a part of the committee that chooses the problems. The bot is present in around 100 servers. We have conducted 30+ seasons with an average participation of over 150 contestants per season!

Dools


It is a 1v1 math competition held among the top 16 contestants of the respective OpenPOTD Season.

Technothlon


I am a problem curator of Technothlon. It is a contest designed to test out-of-the-box-thinking skills of high school students.

These, along with many of my other projects, involved practice of contest-oriented-math and the LaTeX markup language for documentation.
